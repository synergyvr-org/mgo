The quickest wins are built right into the mod list. MGO's [**STEP 3** folder](/start/pre-game#performance-options) offers a few presets. These default to the _performance_ options, but if you've switched to _quality_ and find that your machine is struggling, it's worth considering switching back. The performance presets still look great, and they're significantly easier on your hardware.

* **Community Shaders** has a _Performance_ preset (the default) and a _Quality_ preset. _Quality_ looks better outdoors, but it will cost you some frames.

* **Grass Density** and **LOD Distance** have optional _Quality_ presets too. Leave them unchecked to stay on the lighter settings.

See [Onboarding](/start/pre-game#performance-options) for where to find these in MO2. If you're short on frames, this is the first place to look.
